We recreated the voice of the castrato Farinelli using a deep neural network
trained on seven singers spanning soprano to tenor, embracing the multiplicity
and instability of his historical vocal range. The collaboration produced an
interactive installation, an improvising AI, and a film.

## an·other voice — installation (2023)

<iframe title="vimeo-player" src="https://player.vimeo.com/video/808318370?h=5d249afe97" width="730" height="410" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe>

The synthetic voice was exhibited as the installation *an·other voice* at
[Casino Luxembourg](https://casino-luxembourg.lu/en/agenda/another-voice)
and at the
[Taiwan Contemporary Culture Lab](https://judithdeschamps.com/en/projects/another-voice-taipei).
A presence sensor triggers the voice only when a visitor stands in a specially
designed listening position, mirroring the solitary stance of the king for whom
Farinelli historically performed.

## Farinelli Improviser

A simple AI that improvises a reinterpretation of *Quell'usignolo* by
rearranging small sections according to predefined transitions, using a Markov
chain to move unpredictably across eleven keys.
