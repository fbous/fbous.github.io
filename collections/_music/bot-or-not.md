role: Algorithmic composition
link: https://blaeserphilharmonie-aachen.de/
description: >
  A concert experiment for the Zukunftsforum Aachen2025: two commissioned pieces
  for wind orchestra — one written by a human composer, one generated by an
  algorithm — performed so that neither the orchestra, the conductor nor the
  audience knew which was which until the reveal.
---

The programme also featured a laser harp and was later honoured with a 2019
award from the Sparda-Musiknetzwerk for forward-looking concert programming.
